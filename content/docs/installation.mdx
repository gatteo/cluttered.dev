## System Requirements

Before installing Cluttered, make sure your system meets these requirements:

- **macOS 12 (Monterey) or later**
- Apple Silicon or Intel processor
- 50 MB free disk space
- No admin privileges required

## Download

You can download Cluttered in two formats:

### DMG (Recommended)

1. Download the DMG from our [download page](/download)
2. Open the downloaded `.dmg` file
3. Drag Cluttered to your Applications folder
4. Eject the DMG

### ZIP

1. Download the ZIP from our [download page](/download)
2. Extract the archive
3. Move Cluttered.app to your Applications folder

## First Launch

When you first open Cluttered, macOS may show a warning because the app was downloaded from the internet.

### If you see "App can't be opened"

1. Right-click (or Control-click) on Cluttered.app
2. Select "Open" from the context menu
3. Click "Open" in the dialog

This only needs to be done once. After that, you can open Cluttered normally.

## Granting Permissions

Cluttered needs access to your filesystem to scan for development artifacts.

When prompted:

1. Click "Allow" to grant disk access
2. You may need to go to System Preferences → Security & Privacy → Full Disk Access
3. Enable Cluttered in the list

## Verifying Installation

Once installed, you can verify everything is working:

1. Open Cluttered
2. Click "Scan"
3. You should see a list of detected projects

If you encounter any issues, check our [troubleshooting guide](/docs/troubleshooting) or [contact support](/about).
